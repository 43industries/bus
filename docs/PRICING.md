## Models to consider

|------|----------|------|
| **Pilot** | Single school, limited buses, standard support | Free or nominal fee for case studies |
| **Standard** | N buses, N students cap, diary + map + SMS integration | Annual contract |
| **Plus** | Higher caps, priority support, custom onboarding | Upsell |
