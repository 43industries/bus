The BusBuddy Solution

  • Parents notified automatically when the bus leaves school and arrives at the home stop.

Safety & Reliability

  • No duplicate notifications — unique constraint prevents repeat SMS for the same event.
  • Forward-only status — trip can only progress, never go backwards.
  • GPS stale detection — orange "last seen" indicator if data is older than 2 minutes.
  • Full audit trail — trip_events + notification_log for compliance and accountability.
  • Row-level security — every table locked down; notification logs are service-role only.
